# Health Companion Super App

## Overview

Welcome to the **Health Companion Super App** – a comprehensive UX strategy case study that demonstrates modern design principles applied to healthcare technology. This interactive prototype showcases a user-centered approach to building a health management platform.

## What is this Project?

This project is a detailed exploration of creating an intuitive, accessible health management application. Through comprehensive research, user testing, and iterative design, we've crafted a prototype that prioritizes user needs and accessibility.

## Key Features

### 📋 Comprehensive Dashboard
View your health metrics at a glance, including daily activity, medication schedules, and upcoming appointments.

### 💊 Medication Management
Track your medications with detailed reminders, dosage information, and refill notifications to ensure you never miss a dose.

### 📅 Appointment Scheduling
Manage healthcare appointments seamlessly with calendar integration, reminders, and provider information.

### ⏰ Smart Reminders
Personalized reminders for medications, appointments, and health goals – keeping you on track with your wellness routine.

### ⚙️ Customizable Settings
Tailor the application to your preferences with adjustable notifications, display options, and privacy controls.

## UX Strategy Highlights

This prototype demonstrates:
- **User Research** – Personas and journey mapping based on real user interviews
- **Iterative Design** – Wireframes and prototypes refined through usability testing
- **Accessibility** – WCAG compliance and inclusive design principles
- **Visual Consistency** – Cohesive design system with Tailwind CSS
- **Responsive Design** – Seamless experience across all device sizes
